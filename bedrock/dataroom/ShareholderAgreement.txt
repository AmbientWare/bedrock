# Acme Inc. Shareholder Agreement

This Shareholder Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of April 1, 2015, by and among Acme Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), and the individuals and entities listed on Schedule A attached hereto (each a "Shareholder" and collectively, the "Shareholders").

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Agreement is to provide for the effective management and operation of the Company and to define certain rights and obligations of the Shareholders.

## 2. Board of Directors
2.1 Size: The Board of Directors shall consist of five (5) directors.
2.2 Nomination Rights: 
   - Founder Shareholders shall have the right to nominate three (3) directors.
   - Series A Investors shall have the right to nominate one (1) director.
   - One (1) director shall be an independent director mutually agreed upon by the Founder Shareholders and Series A Investors.

## 3. Transfer Restrictions
3.1 Right of First Refusal: Before any Shareholder may transfer any shares, they must first offer them to the Company and then to the other Shareholders.
3.2 Tag-Along Rights: If a Shareholder proposes to sell more than 50% of their shares, other Shareholders have the right to participate in the sale on a pro-rata basis.
3.3 Drag-Along Rights: If Shareholders holding at least 75% of the shares propose to sell all their shares, they can require the other Shareholders to join the sale on the same terms.

## 4. Pre-emptive Rights
Shareholders shall have the right to participate pro-rata in any new issuance of shares by the Company, subject to customary exceptions.

## 5. Information Rights
The Company shall provide Shareholders with annual audited financial statements, quarterly unaudited financial statements, and an annual budget.

## 6. Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation
Shareholders agree not to compete with the Company or solicit its employees or customers for a period of two years following the termination of their relationship with the Company.

## 7. Intellectual Property
All intellectual property developed by Shareholders related to the Company's business shall be assigned to the Company.

## 8. Dividends
The declaration and payment of dividends shall be at the discretion of the Board of Directors, subject to applicable law and the Company's financial condition.

## 9. Exit Strategy
9.1 The Shareholders agree to work in good faith towards a liquidity event (IPO or sale of the Company) within 7-10 years from the date of this Agreement.
9.2 Any liquidity event must be approved by Shareholders holding at least 66% of the outstanding shares.

## 10. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Delaware, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

## 11. Amendments
This Agreement may be amended only with the written consent of the Company and Shareholders holding at least 75% of the outstanding shares.

## 12. Termination
This Agreement shall terminate upon the closing of the Company's initial public offering or upon the sale of all or substantially all of the Company's assets or stock.
