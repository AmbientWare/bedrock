# Acme Inc. Environmental Assessments Summary

## 1. Executive Summary

This document summarizes the environmental assessments conducted for Acme Inc.'s properties and operations. Key findings include:

- No significant environmental liabilities identified at owned properties
- Manufacturing facilities operate within permitted emissions limits
- Ongoing monitoring and improvement of waste management practices
- Commitment to reducing carbon footprint across all operations
- Some areas identified for potential improvement in energy efficiency

## 6. Sustainability Goals and Progress

1. **Carbon Neutrality:** 
   - Goal: Achieve carbon neutrality in operations by 2030
   - Progress: 20% reduction in emissions since 2019 baseline
2. **Renewable Energy:** 
   - Goal: 100% renewable energy for all facilities by 2025
   - Progress: Currently at 75% renewable energy use
3. **Zero Waste:** 
   - Goal: Zero waste to landfill from manufacturing operations by 2027
   - Progress: Currently diverting 85% of waste from landfill
4. **Water Conservation:** 
   - Goal: Reduce water consumption by 30% by 2028 (from 2020 baseline)
   - Progress: 15% reduction achieved as of 2023

## 7. Recommendations for Future Assessments

1. Conduct comprehensive biodiversity impact assessments for all major facilities
2. Perform lifecycle assessments (LCA) for key products to identify areas for sustainability improvements
3. Engage in more detailed Scope 3 emissions mapping and reduction planning
4. Assess climate change risks and adaptation strategies for all major facilities
